The best ice creams in Colombia

Why ice cream?

Ice cream is one of the most consumed desserts in the world. New zealend and the United States are the countries that consumed more ice cream per capita in the world.

Ice cream (derived from earlier iced cream or cream ice) is a sweetened frozen food typically eaten as a snack or dessert. It may be made from dairy milk or cream and is flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and any spice, such as cocoa or vanilla. Colourings are usually added, in addition to stabilizers. The mixture is stirred to incorporate air spaces and cooled below the freezing point of water to prevent detectable ice crystals from forming. The result is a smooth, semi-solid foam that is solid at very low temperatures (below 2 °C or 35 °F). It becomes more malleable as its temperature increases.

Best places for Ice cream in Colombia

Crepes & Waffles

Crepes & Waflles is the number one restaurant on sales in Colombia. It is well renowned by its crepes and desserts, and you will find flavors that you wont't find anywhere.

Popsy

Popsy is one of the favorite creamerys of colombians. They offer 26 different flavours, some of them are special mixes with chocolate bars like Milky Way or Snickers.

Michelle

Michelle is a gelato style creamery, they make their ice cream everyday and it is 100% natural.

Orso Heladería

Orso is focus on the quality of its ice creams, they offer flavours from all over the world and make them with italian recipes.

GoYurt

GoYurt is a well renowned place for yogurt ice cream, they care about the nutritional component of its products and they offer different toppings that you can add to your ice cream.
